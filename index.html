Personal Projects

Ducking Bread – Torment NG++

03.2021 - 06.2021

"Ducking Bread" is a dynamic mobile game where players take on the role of a caretaker of a pond, attempting to deter ducks from unhealthy food and guide them towards healthier treats. "Ducking Bread" not only provides entertainment but also educates players about healthy nutrition for wild birds, promoting ecological awareness and care for nature.

Top3 in "Mobile Games" Category with special awards from:

  • Render Cube

"Ducking Bread" was a project developed in Unity, where I served as the programmer, level designer, gameplay designer, and was responsible for creating shaders such as water, grass, outlines, and snow effects. As the programmer, I was tasked with implementing game mechanics, user interface elements, and ensuring smooth gameplay functionality. In my role as a level designer, I crafted engaging and challenging levels, considering player progression and difficulty curve. As the gameplay designer, I conceptualized and fine-tuned the core mechanics of the game, balancing player interactions and challenges to create an enjoyable experience. Additionally, I was responsible for iterating on gameplay elements based on user feedback and playtesting results. Creating shaders was another aspect of my role, where I utilized Unity's Shader Graph or wrote custom shaders to achieve visual effects such as realistic water ripples, dynamic grass movement, distinct outlines for game objects, and immersive snowfall effects. Overall, my multi-faceted involvement in the development of "Ducking Bread" allowed me to contribute to various aspects of the game's creation, from its fundamental programming to its visual aesthetics, resulting in a cohesive and engaging gaming experience.
